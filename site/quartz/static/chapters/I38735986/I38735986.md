## Tobl Zitserman Introduction

[Tobl](/profiles/Tobl-Hochman-%28Hoffman%29-Zitserman) (Tubb'l) was the wife of [Moshe Hoffman](/profiles/Moshe-%D7%9E%D7%A9%D7%94-Hoffman-Hochman) and mother of their children.


For more information about her life, see the chapter about her in [Moshe Hoffman's biography](/profiles/Moshe-%D7%9E%D7%A9%D7%94-Hoffman-Hochman#chapter=07-tobl-zitserman&tab=biography).
